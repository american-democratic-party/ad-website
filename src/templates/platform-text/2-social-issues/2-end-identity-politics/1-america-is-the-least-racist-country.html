America is the Least Racist Country in the World

Of the almost 200 countries in the world, the U.S. is the single least racist country in the entire world.  Racism will never be 100% solved in any country, but America has achieved the greatest diversity of successful people on the planet. 

We are the only country to produce self-made billionaires from all major racial groups.  American politicians and thought leaders come from all walks of life, and our strong freedom of speech protection applies to all ethnicities.
